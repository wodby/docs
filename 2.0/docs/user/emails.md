# Emails

Manage your email addresses from `User settings > Emails`.

The page shows which address is primary and whether each address is verified.

## What you can do

- add a secondary email
- verify a newly added email
- promote a verified secondary email to primary
- delete a non-primary email

## Important rules

- only verified secondary emails can become the primary email
- the primary email cannot be deleted directly
