# Account

The `Account` page in user settings is the basic profile page for your Wodby user.

## What you can do

- update your display name
- delete your account

## Deleting your account

Account deletion is handled from the same page and should be treated as a permanent action. Review organization membership, access, and automation that depends on your user before deleting it.
